At a Glance


Students Science Association was established on November 14,2009. Presently there are about 10 plus life members besides student members. Life members are mainly teachers of schools, colleges and university of Tripura. Go To top

Objectives

  1. Popularization of Mathematics.
  2. Eradication of Mathematics phobia, if any, among the students.
  3. To create interest about Mathematics among the learners.
  4. To inspire students to have joy of learning Mathematics.
  5. To unfold beauties, excitement and fun in Mathematics.
  6. Promotion and creation of awareness of Mathematics and Mathematical studies among the learners and teachers.
  7. To identify a group of talents in mathematics.
  8. To motivate teachers to have pleasure of teaching mathematics.
  9. Creation of research environment in Mathematics in Tripura.
  10. To highlight rich Indian heritage and culture of mathematical sciences
